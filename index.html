What not to do

Because of the Covid-19 pandemic, it is important that we all quarantine. That doesn't mean we cannot picnic, it just means we cant be near others. So, do not invite any friends to this picnic. To be honest there are many things you shouldn't do, like jump out the window. But the main thing here is staying safe.

What to do

You should make sure you bring the necessary materials and prepare. Make sure to check the weather and go on a sunny day, also make sure to bring hand sanitizer so that you can rid your hand of germs before you eat. Talking about eating, definetly bring sadnwhiches. Making Sandwhiches

To make a sandwhich you get any amount of slices of bread you want as long as its more than one and is a whole number of bread(no halfes). Put anything eatable in between the slices of bread as long as it's eatable and you enjoy it and is meant to be on a sadwhich like this
